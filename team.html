🌟 What Makes Our Teams Special

🔧 Scrappy Attitude, Big Vision

Each season's team operates like a small startup, with each member leading their part of planning and execution.

🤝 Teamwork & Leadership

Every member contributes unique skills to build themselves and each other up year over year.

🏆 Competition Excellence

Teams compete at regional and state levels, with opportunities to advance to world championships.

💼 Team Roles & Skills

Our teams are structured to provide diverse learning opportunities:

🔩 Mechanical Engineering

Design and build robot chassis, mechanisms, and moving parts

💻 Programming

Code autonomous routines, sensor integration, and driver controls

⚡ Electrical

Wire motors, sensors, and control systems safely and efficiently

📊 Strategy & Scouting

Analyze competition data and develop winning game strategies

🎨 Design & CAD

Create 3D models, prototypes, and technical documentation

📢 Outreach & Marketing

Community engagement, social media, and team presentation
